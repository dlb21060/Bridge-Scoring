Scoring Format for Manual Entry


Scores may be entered manually, instead of using the drop-down menu options.

The format for scoring is:

LEVEL DENOMINATION [optional double] PLAYER RESULT
-- OR --
PASS

LEVEL is designated by 1,2,...7
DESIGNATION is one of C, D, H, S, or N
DOUBLE may be blank, X (for a doubled contract) or XX (for a redoubled contract)
PLAYER is N, E, S, or W
RESULT is = for contracts made without overtricks, +{1-6} for contracts making overtricks, or -{1-13} for contracts going down

Examples:

The hand is passed out
PASS

4 Hearts by South, making exactly
4HS=

4 Spades by North, doubled, down one
4SxN-1

1 No Trump by East, redoubled, making with one overtrick
1NxxE+1

3 No Trump by West, making
3 N W =

Note that spaces between elements of the score are optional, and case is irrelevant.
Thus, 4SxN-1 is equivalent to: 4 s X n -1
